How HossAgent Works

Three simple steps to turn AI into a measurable profit center.

📋
STEP 1

Queue Tasks with Rewards

Define tasks and assign a reward value in cents. Each task becomes a paid job for your AI worker.

⚡
STEP 2

Agent Executes Autonomously

HossAgent uses LLM + tools (search, fetch, analyze) to complete tasks without human intervention.

📈
STEP 3

Track Cost & Profit

The dashboard shows exact token cost, reward earned, and real profit per task and in aggregate.
