What This Is

A prototype of an autonomous business.

Four agents — BizDev, Onboarding, Ops, Billing — share one data model. The system discovers leads, converts them into customers, executes their work, and generates invoices automatically.

Key Components:

• Lead generation & qualification
• Customer onboarding & task creation
• Autonomous task execution
• Billing & invoice management
• Real-time profit tracking

Why It Matters

Manual Work Scales Poorly

The cost of hiring knowledge workers rises. AI tools don't solve the problem—they sit disconnected from revenue.

AI Lacks Business Discipline

Most AI deployments don't track cost per task. Nobody knows the actual ROI. HossAgent calculates profit in real time.

Autonomy Requires Accountability

A system that generates leads, completes tasks, and produces invoices—without human intervention—must prove its value immediately.
