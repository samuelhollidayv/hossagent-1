How to Use HossAgent

A field manual for operators who want movement, not dashboards.

HossAgent is built to remove the part of business everyone hates: hunting for opportunities, chasing leads, and doing the repetitive work that steals your time. This guide shows you exactly how to use it — simply, quickly, and without babysitting.

1

Set Up Your Business Profile

Train HossAgent on what you do, who you serve, and your tone. This defines your ICP and your voice.

2

Choose Autopilot or Review Mode

  • Autopilot: Hoss runs end-to-end.
  • Review Mode: Hoss drafts; you approve.
3

Understand LeadEvents

LeadEvents are moments — not contacts. Each one is a real-world trigger: a bad review, a hiring signal, a shift in demand, a competitive move. Hoss turns these into actionable opportunities.

4

Watch Outreach Happen Automatically

Drafted emails based on timing, context, and relevance.

  • In Autopilot Mode: sends automatically.
  • In Review Mode: waits for your approval.
5

View Reports and Market Snapshots

Hoss generates research and insights, delivered directly into your portal.

6

Track Progress in Your Dashboard

See opportunities, outbound, trial status, usage, and wins.

7

Manage Your Subscription

Upgrade, cancel, reactivate — cleanly and simply.

8

Optional: Connect Real Outbound Sending

Configure SendGrid/SMTP if you want to send real email. DRY_RUN mode remains available.

9

Daily Use

You check the portal. Hoss handles the chase. Your business keeps moving.

10

While You Sleep

The Autopilot Loop continues generating: signals, LeadEvents, outreach, insights. You wake up to motion.

HossAgent: growth without grind. Motion without extra hours. Opportunities without the chase.
